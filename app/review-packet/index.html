What to judge

Patient/helper lens
Plain wording, visible not-sure options, and no pressure to guess.
Nurse lens
Completion support, missing information, medication review, bladder diary, and containment cues.
Clinician lens
Main concern, duration, pattern, priority review statements, context, and missing information in under one minute.
Governance lens
Safety boundary, workflow fit, staff burden, existing process gap, and smallest next artifact.

Decision rules

Outcome

Continue

Use when workflow pain is clear, the summary would be read, a slot exists, staff burden is acceptable, and safety is acceptable.

Revise

Use when the idea fits but wording, summary format, or workflow placement needs another pass.

Narrow

Use when value exists only for assisted completion, selected patients, or one symptom module.

Pause

Use when safety, workflow slot, staff burden, summary usefulness, or existing-process value blocks the MVP.
